Reliance undergraduate Scholarship

Reliance Foundation is an Indian non-profit organisation which was founded in 2010 by Mukesh Ambani.


Scholarship features
1. Support meritorious students from all corners of the country with an undergraduate college education
2. For first year students studying any stream of their choice
3. Awarded on a merit-cum-means basis to meritorious students
4. Up to 5,000 undergraduate scholars will be selected
5. The total amount of scholarship available over the duration of the degree programme, will be up to Rs/INR 2 Lakhs
6. Scholarship will go beyond financial support, with students automatically gaining networking opportunities through a strong alumni network

Application and Selection Process
1. Students will start the application process by clicking on the link given below.
2. All applicants to answer the online aptitude test. Test duration will be 60 mins and will contain 60 Multiple Choice Questions. Components of the test include verbal ability, analytical and logical ability and numerical ability.
3. Applications will be evaluated using a combination of aptitude test score, academic and personal information.

Eligibility Criteria
1. Be a resident Indian citizen
2. Passed Standard 12th with minimum 60% and is attending a full-time undergraduate degree programme In India
3. Students having House Hold income < Rs. 15 Lakhs (preference given to < Rs. 2.5 Lakhs)
4. Answering the mandatory Aptitude Test
