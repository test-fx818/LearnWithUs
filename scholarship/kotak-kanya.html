Kotak Kanya Scholarship 2023

Kotak Kanya Scholarship programme provides financial assistance to meritorious girl students from underserved communities from all across India to complete their higher education.
Along with the scholarship, key features of the program include mentorship support, communicative English training and academic support required for holistic development

Important Dates

  • Application start date: 12th July 2023
  • Application Deadline : 30th September

Eligibility Criteria

  • Open for meritorious girl students across India
  • Applicants must have scored 85% or more marks or equivalent CGPA in Class 12 board examinations
  • Applicant’s annual family income must be below INR. 6,00,000 (Rupees Six Lakh)
  • Meritorious girl students who have secured admission to first-year graduation programs in the Academic Year - 2023 from institute of repute (NIRF/NAAC accredited) for professional graduation courses like Engineering I MBBS I Integrated LLB (5 years) or other professional courses (Design, Architecture, etc.)
  • Children of employees of Kotak Mahindra Group, Kotak Education Foundation & Buddy4Study are not eligible to apply for Kotak Kanya Scholarship 2023

Perks

  • Scholarship of Rs. 1.5 lakh* per year will be given to each selected scholar till the completion of her professional graduation course/degree.

Application Process

  • Click on the ‘Apply Now’ button below.
  • Login to Buddy4Study using a registered ID to land on the ‘Online Application Form Page’
  • You will now be redirected to the ‘Kotak Kanya Scholarship 2023’ application form page.
  • Click on the ‘Start Application’ button to begin the application process
  • Fill in the required details in the online scholarship application form.
  • Upload the required documents.
  • Accept the ‘Terms and Conditions’ and click on ‘Preview’.
  • If all the details filled in by the applicant are correctly showing on the preview screen, click on the ‘Submit’ button to complete the application process.
